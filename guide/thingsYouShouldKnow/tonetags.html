ToneTags:

Credit to: FR regiment and Wikipedia.

 "A tone indicator or tone tag is a symbol attached to a sentence or message sent in a textual form,
such as over the internet, to explicitly state the intonation or intent of the message, especially when it may be otherwise ambiguous."

Here is a list of tonetags and what they mean:

Commonly used Tone tags.

These Tone tags are commonly used on Ponytown and other regiment that supports ToneTags:

/j : Joke

Means that what you just said was a joke and should be took at 2nd degree.

/hj : Half joke

means that what you just said was a half joke.

/srs : serious

Mean that what you just say is serious and should be took at the 1st degree
Variant - /vsrs : Very serious

/info or /gen: information or genuine

You want to inform someone about something.
Variant - /genq : genuine question -> that question is good to ask at the moment.

/lh: light hearted

A definition would be welcomed

/nf : not forcing

Mean that the person will not force you to answer if you don't want.
