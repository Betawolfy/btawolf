 "Ponytown regiment use lot of abreviations in order to not take lot of time while speaking."

Here is a list of some abreviations and what they mean:

PTA or PTAFK : permission to afk

asking permission to temporary be afk. You can't be afk for all the activity.
⚠️ - If you are AFK, be sure to be in formal! you'll receive a warning if you afk in combat!

PTD: permission to dismiss:

Ask permission to leave the activity early.
